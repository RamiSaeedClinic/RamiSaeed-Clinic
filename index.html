About Dr. Rami Said

Dr. Rami Said is a board-certified general surgeon specializing in hepatobiliary, pancreatic, and bariatric surgery. With years of training and experience, he has performed hundreds of successful laparoscopic procedures and complex abdominal operations.

Our Services

  • Liver Surgery: Tumors, cysts, hydatid disease, bile duct repair
  • Pancreatic Surgery: Tumors, pancreatitis, Whipple procedures
  • Gallbladder & Biliary: Gallstone removal, bile duct injury repair
  • Bariatric Surgery: Sleeve gastrectomy, mini gastric bypass, revisional surgery
