About Dr. Rami Said

Dr. Rami Said is an Associate professor & board-certified general digestive surgeon specializing in hepatobiliary, liver transplantation,laparoscopic , upper & lower GI and bariatric surgery. With years of training and experience, he has performed hundreds of successful laparoscopic procedures and complex abdominal operations.

Our Services

  • Liver Surgery & liver transplantation: Tumors, cysts, hydatid disease, bile duct repair
  • Pancreatic Surgery: Tumors, pancreatitis, Whipple procedures
  • Gallbladder & Biliary: Laparoscopic cholecystectomy, cholangiocarcinomas , obstructive jaundice treatment & bile duct injury repair
  • Bariatric Surgery: Sleeve gastrectomy, mini gastric bypass, revisional surgery , Intragastric balloon & Elipse capsule
  • Laparoscopic Surgery: Appendectomy,antireflux surgery,splenectomy,cardiomyotomy,
  • Upper GI & Colorectal surgery
